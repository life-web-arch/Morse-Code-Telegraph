1. Overview

Morse Code Telegraph ("we", "our", or "the site") operates at morse-code-telegraph.vercel.app. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the site.

In short: we do not collect, store, or sell your personal data. All core functionality of the Morse Code Telegraph tool (translation, audio generation, audio decoding) runs entirely inside your browser.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not require you to create an account.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information during normal use.
  • Text you type into the translator and any audio files you upload are processed locally in your browser and are never sent to our servers.
  • We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

3. Local Storage

The site may store a single preference ("morseTheme") in your browser's localStorage to remember your dark/light theme selection between visits. This data never leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services which may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

  • Vercel (hosting) — may log request IP addresses and headers as part of standard server infrastructure. See Vercel Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — used to load web fonts. Requests go to Google's servers. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Advertising — this site displays third-party advertisements (Adsterra / Highperformanceformat). These ad networks may use cookies, device identifiers, and similar tracking technologies to serve personalised ads. We do not control these trackers. You can opt out via your browser's ad settings or a tool like aboutads.info.

5. Analytics

We do not currently run first-party analytics on this site. If we add analytics in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect that.

6. Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use the Contact page.
